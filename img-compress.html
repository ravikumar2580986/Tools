How Our Image Compressor Works

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce your image file size while maintaining visual quality. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the browse button
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = more compression)
  3. Select your desired output format (keep original or convert to JPG, PNG, WebP)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Smaller images load faster, use less bandwidth, and improve your website's SEO ranking. Our compressor is perfect for:
